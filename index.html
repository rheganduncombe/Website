Dolphins

Female dolphins are called cows
Males are called bulls
Young dolphins are called calves

Dolphins are believed to be very intelligent

Dolphins live in schools or pods of up to 12 individuals.



Most species live in shallow areas in tropical and temperate oceans throughout the world. Five species live in the world's rivers.

Dolphins are part of the family of whales that includes orcas and pilot whales. Killer whales are actually dolphins.
